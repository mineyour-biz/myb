MYB Phone - First Boot

To decrease the likelihood of leaking any usable private information or metadata:

  • DO NOT INSTALL A SIM CARD for initial set up! 
  • Connect your phone to a wifi network away from your home or workplace.
  • Turn the phone on.
  • Accept permissions.
  • Turn on a VPN before doing anything more.
    • CalyxVPN is preinstalled for this purpose- simply press the button to activate & your IP address is protected for the remainder of set up.

 

  • After you've set up CalyxVPN (or installed custom VPN configurations), feel free to add your SIM card or connect to familiar wifi access points.
  • Open f-droid to search for application updates & new apps you'd like to use.

 

  • If you want to use apps from Google Play - find them in the Aurora Store instead.
  • You are ready to start using the built-in privacy features of your phone!
